Speedinvest scaled from a modest first fund to €1.2B across six EMEA offices by implementing automatic relationship tracking systems. Rather than relying on manual data entry and memory, the firm uses automated activity capture to monitor LP interactions, flag stale relationships after 60 days without contact, and provide firm-wide visibility into their fundraising pipeline.
